Projects

UXCam

UXCam is a mobile app analytics and user experience (UX) tool designed to help developers and product teams understand how users interact with their mobile applications. It provides insights into user behavior by recording and analyzing user sessions.

I contributed to the migration from a legacy system (in PHP) to a new microservice architecture (in Python). Additionally, I redesigned the architecture and implemented a subscription management system, improved the stability of the authentication service, and implemented seamless event tracking for both in-house systems and third-party libraries like Mixpanel, Intercom, and HubSpot.

Merojob

Merojob is the largest job site in Nepal with over 300+ real-time users, more than half a million registered users, and 100,000 daily page visits.

As a Software Developer, I was responsible for developing various features and optimizing existing ones. I designed and developed features like Job Search, Job Apply, Jobseeker Search, and Application Shortlisting. Additionally, I developed reports and analytics pages, implemented caching, and created REST APIs for the mobile app.

Rojgari

Rojgari is a job portal for blue-collar workers under UK Aid with over 30,000 registered users.

As a Software Engineer, I was responsible for designing the overall architecture and developing REST APIs. I also handled deployment and CI/CD implementation.

RealHRSoft

RealHRSoft provides HR software that combines technology with effortless Cloud HRIS solutions covering the entire spectrum from recruitment to separation processes.

I was involved in architecture design and developed the backend for modules including HRIS, Attendance and Leave, Permission, Notifications, Noticeboard, and Reports. I also maintained and enhanced modules like Payroll, Task, and Events, along with deployment and CI/CD implementation.
